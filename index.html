Recast — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

Recast is an audio player app that plays files from local storage, iCloud Drive, and SMB network shares. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Recast does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

Data Stored on Device

  • Folder configurations — Paths and bookmarks for folders you add (stored in UserDefaults)
  • SMB credentials — Server address, username, and password for SMB connections (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • Playback history — Last playback position per folder and played episode status (stored in UserDefaults)
  • Folder artwork — Custom artwork images you set for folders (stored in app Documents directory)

Network Access

Recast connects to SMB network shares only when you explicitly configure and initiate a connection. No other network requests are made by the app itself.

Third-Party Services

Recast may include Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party SDKs are used.

Children's Privacy

Recast does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
